Sunday Gatherings

Join us this Sunday at 4pm or 5:30pm

Why we meet when we meet

We know how busy Silicon Valley life can be and how important weekend rest, relaxation, and fun is for a balanced life. We intentionally gather as a church community on Sunday evenings to allow for maximum optimization of our weekends. We encourage you to sleep in, spend time with friends & family, or take a weekend trip; and then start your new week with community, worship, and the Word of God at Garden City Church.

We have Kids’ Ministry during the 4 pm service. The nursery is open to babies ages 4 months to walking and is located off the sanctuary. Our Kids’ Center is located across the lawn near the playground. We have a walker room for babies up to 24 months old and classes for kids ages 2 through 5th grade. In the sanctuary, we offer reserved seating in the back for parents with young children and a TV in the lobby so parents and their young children can participate in the worship service. For more information on Kids’ Ministry email Haley Hatai.
